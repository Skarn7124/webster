About Pikadex:

Pikadex is a task Managing Platform where you can create, delete or edit task and even track your progress through ghantt chart.It's features are ...

  • Stay organized with subtasks

    Track important tasks with the Watch features.

  • Track progress visually

    Features for accurate tracking and process optimization.

  • Task hierarchy

    Break down large projects and tasks into subtasks to move work forward in logical stages.

  • Custom fields

    Create custom task templates, so you can generate common tasks quickly.

  • Issue templates

    Standardize issue creation by creating templates.

  • Notifications

    Notify the right people about the updates they need.
